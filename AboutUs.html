About So-Cal Capital

A Commitment to Quality

Established in 2010 and fully realized in 2015, SO-CAL Capital led by a team of experienced real estate professionals, who bring depth and range to the Business Purpose Loan market.

With a focus on maximizing returns via common sense underwriting, we approach each transaction with the goal of creating long-term, results-oriented relationships within our network of investors, mortgage brokers, and real estate partners.

Deep Client Relations

Central to SO-CAL's business practice is our appreciated commitment to exceeding expectations at every level for our clients.

Thorough Vetting

From initial contact, through vetting and funding, our team members strive to provide superior service at every level.

Diversity of Products

Our team provides a wide range of products to keep you excited about your investments, which has defined our fast growing boutique firm.
