Leadership

Unparalleled experience facilitating over one billion dollars in transaction funding, along with personal commitments to foster reliable, trustworthy and profitable relationships define SO-CAL Capital’s leadership team.

Jaclyn Perham

President

With the founding of SO-CAL Capital 2010, Jaclyn Perham drew upon her deeply rooted love for the real estate industry combined with a personal mission to help clients grow their individual portfolios through informed and thought-out opportunities.

A graduate of the University of Southern California, as well as a licensed California Real Estate Broker for over a decade, Perham has built a professional reputation for integrity and creativity in her approach to investment strategies. Dedicated and entrepreneurial by nature, she is committed to approaching each investment with an underwriting principal based upon informed matrixes and deep industry know-how.

In her role as President and CEO of SO-CAL Capital, Inc., Perham has set the firm on a path that includes not only expanding its' funding base but developing a real estate investment fund and broadening its’ portfolio of investment opportunities.

A Southern California native and current resident of Costa Mesa, Ms. Perham is an active participant in numerous community organizations.

Austin Glidewell

Vice-President

An experienced professional committed to results with integrity, Austin Glidewell, serves as Vice President of SO-CAL Capital, Inc. with a charter to establish the firm as a premier destination for real estate investing.

Beginning with his first position with a full-service real estate private equity group in Scottsdale, Arizona, Glidewell’s career has evolved over a series of roles focused on real estate investing through acquisitions and portfolio management. A stint with a private capital firm in Orange County, California, overseeing nearly half a billion dollars in residential assets led him to his current role with SO-CAL Capital.

An articulate and staunch believer in “doing the right thing even when no one is watching,” Glidewell operates under an often-lauded professional work ethic focused on placing investor’s interests first.

A graduate of Arizona State University, with a Juris Doctorate from Arizona Summit Law School, Glidewell currently resides in Irvine, California with his wife and three sons.
